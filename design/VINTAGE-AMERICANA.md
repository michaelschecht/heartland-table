## Typography

### Display (Titles)
- **Font:** League Gothic or Bebas Neue
- **Weight:** 700 (Bold)
- **Style:** Bold, condensed, all-caps
- **Letter-spacing:** +0.03em
- **Usage:** Book title, big headlines

### Headings
- **Font:** Rockwell or Courier Bold
- **Weight:** 700 (Bold)
- **Style:** Sturdy slab serif, typewriter aesthetic
- **Usage:** Recipe titles, section headers

### Body Text
- **Font:** American Typewriter or Courier
- **Weight:** 400 (Regular)
- **Line-height:** 1.5
- **Style:** Nostalgic, readable, typewriter feel
- **Usage:** Instructions, ingredients, descriptions

## Visual Style Guidelines

### Photography
- **Filter:** Warm, slightly oversaturated vintage tones
- **Contrast:** High, bold shadows
- **Plating:** Retro-style presentation (1950s-60s aesthetic)
- **Props:** Vintage dishware, gingham tablecloths, enamelware
- **Processing:** Slight grain/texture overlay

### Decorative Elements

#### Borders & Frames
- **Thickness:** 3-5px, bold and visible
- **Style:** Geometric, straight lines and right angles
- **Colors:** Alternating red, blue, yellow
- **Patterns:** Stripes, checkered (gingham), stars

#### Badges & Ribbons
- **Shapes:** Circles, shields, ribbon banners, starbursts
- **Usage:** Section markers, callouts, awards/ratings
- **Colors:** Red with yellow accents or blue with white text
- **Style:** Flat design with bold outlines

#### Icons & Symbols
- **Stars:** ★ scattered throughout
- **Ribbons:** Curved banner shapes
- **Checkered:** Gingham pattern accents
- **Wheat/grain:** Agricultural motifs

### Typography Treatments
- **All-caps:** Frequent use for emphasis
- **Letter-spacing:** Generous, especially in headings (+0.05-0.1em)
- **Text shadows:** Subtle, for depth (1px offset)
- **Outlines:** White or yellow stroke around text on colored backgrounds

### Spacing
- **Margins:** 0.375in (tighter than modern style)
- **Padding:** 0.75-1em around boxes
- **Line spacing:** 1.5 for body text
- **Element spacing:** 1-1.5em between sections

### Color Usage Rules
- **Text on light:** Deep charcoal (#212121)
- **Text on red:** White or butter cream
- **Text on blue:** White or pale yellow
- **Text on yellow:** Deep charcoal or red
- **Borders:** Primary colors (red/blue) or accent (yellow)
- **Backgrounds:** Butter cream base with colored accent boxes

### Textures & Effects
- **Paper grain:** Subtle at 5-10% opacity
- **Halftone dots:** Very light, simulating old printing
- **Color separation:** Slight RGB offset for retro print feel (optional)
- **Noise:** Light grain for vintage authenticity
- **No gradients:** Flat colors only, true to era

### Patterns
- **Gingham checkered:** Red/white or blue/white, 8-10px squares
- **Stripes:** Bold horizontal or vertical, 20-30px width
- **Stars:** Scattered decoratively, not overwhelming
- **Dots:** Polka dots in accent areas (yellow on red, etc.)

## Mood & Feel
- Nostalgic, playful, approachable
- Bold and unapologetic with color
- Fun, celebratory, community-oriented
- County fair, state fair, main street USA
- Diner menus, vintage advertisements
- Patriotic without being overtly political
- Warm, inviting, familiar

### Decorative Elements
- **Star bullets:** Use `★` (U+2605) or `☆` (U+2606)
- **Dividers:** Thick `═` borders or colored stripes
- **Corners:** Square, no border-radius (except badges)
- **Shadows:** Flat offset shadows (no blur), 3-4px offset
