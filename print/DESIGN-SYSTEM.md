## Typography

### Display (Titles, Chapter Headers)
- **Font:** Playfair Display
- **Weight:** Bold (700)
- **Size:** 36-48pt

### Recipe Titles
- **Font:** Playfair Display
- **Weight:** SemiBold (600)
- **Size:** 24-28pt
- **Color:** Rustic Red `#A0522D`

### Body Text
- **Font:** Source Serif Pro
- **Weight:** Regular (400)
- **Size:** 11pt
- **Line Height:** 1.5
- **Color:** Deep Brown `#3D2B1F`

### UI/Labels (At a Glance, Tips)
- **Font:** Source Sans Pro
- **Weight:** Regular/SemiBold
- **Size:** 9-10pt
- **Color:** Charcoal `#2C2C2C`

### Ingredient Lists
- **Font:** Source Sans Pro
- **Weight:** Regular
- **Size:** 10pt
- **Line Height:** 1.6

## Print Production Notes

1. **Color Mode:** CMYK for print
2. **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum for all images
3. **Fonts:** Embed all fonts or convert to outlines
4. **PDF/X-1a** or **PDF/X-4** for print-ready export
5. **Page numbering:** Roman numerals for front matter, Arabic for content
6. **Bleeds:** Extend background colors/images 0.125" past trim
