## Two Core Directions

### Concept 1: Rustic Modern Farmhouse

**Vibe:** Fresh, inviting, elevated take on Midwestern charm. Blends classic farmhouse with clean modern design.

**Emotional Response:** Cherished memories, home-cooked goodness with a bright, approachable feel. Cozy without being quaint, nostalgic yet current.

**Color System:**
- **Primary:** Creamy whites, oatmeals, muted light greys
- **Accents:** Soft sage greens, dusty blues, warm terracotta, deep berry reds (Michigan cherries, apple orchards)

**Typography:**
- **Display:** Playfair Display or Lora (sophisticated, heritage feel)
- **Body:** Merriweather or Baskerville (comfortable, readable)
- **UI/Accents:** Lato or Open Sans (modern contrast)

**Photography:**
- Soft natural window light, airy and bright
- "Perfectly imperfect" styling
- Wooden cutting boards, simple ceramic bowls, cast iron, linen napkins
- Mix of overhead flat lays and intimate close-ups

**Print Production:**
- Uncoated or lightly coated matte stock (tactile, natural feel)
- Hardcover with matte laminate, debossed title
- Possible partial cloth binding (linen-texture spine)
- Smyth-sewn for lay-flat

---

### Concept 2: Heritage & Hearty (Classic Comfort)

**Vibe:** Rich, warm, nostalgic, deeply authentic. Like a treasured family heirloom.

**Emotional Response:** Family history, generous hospitality, satisfying fullness of home-cooked meals.

**Color System:**
- **Primary:** Rich browns (aged wood), forest greens, deep creams
- **Accents:** Rustic reds (worn barn), mustard yellows, deep blues (faded denim, old enamel)

**Typography:**
- **Display:** Old Standard TT or Georgia (tradition, reliability)
- **Body:** Bookman Old Style or Adobe Garamond Pro
- **Accents:** Warm hand-drawn script for "Grandma's Secret"

**Photography:**
- Warm, moody lighting (late afternoon sun, cozy indoor glow)
- Abundant, generous compositions with deeper shadows
- Vintage serving dishes, checkered tablecloths, flour-dusted countertops
- Close-ups emphasizing texture, steam, hearty nature

**Print Production:**
- Coated stock with subtle sheen (dull/silk) for rich color pop
- Hardcover with full-color dust jacket
- Spot UV on title/graphics
- Ribbon bookmark

---

## Recommended Hybrid: Modern Rustic Farmhouse with Heritage Accents

The designer recommends combining:
- **Base aesthetic:** Modern Rustic Farmhouse (clean, fresh, contemporary)
- **Special sections:** Heritage Accents (nostalgic, personal touches)

## Print Production Options

### Small Runs (100 copies): Print-on-Demand
- No upfront inventory
- Higher per-unit cost
- Limited customization
- Best for: test runs, gifts, niche markets

### Large Runs (1000+ copies): Offset Printing
- Lower per-unit cost at scale
- Superior quality & consistency
- Full customization options
- Best for: retail distribution, premium product
