The measurements on these cards follow a system that predates the metric system, the imperial system, and in some cases, written language itself.

We present, for the first time in print, the **Official Midwest Measurement Conversion Chart.**

## The Golden Rule

When in doubt: **more is more.** No Midwesterner has ever been criticized for making too much food. Running out of food at a gathering, however, is a scandal that will be discussed in hushed tones for years.
