## Theme 2: **Vintage Americana**
*1950s diner meets county fair*

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** #D32F2F (Cherry Red)
- **Secondary:** #1976D2 (Classic Blue)
- **Accent:** #FBC02D (Mustard Yellow)
- **Text:** #212121 (Deep Charcoal)
- **Background:** #FFFDE7 (Butter Cream)
- **Highlight:** #FFE082 (Pale Yellow)

### Typography
- **Display:** League Gothic or Bebas Neue (bold, condensed)
- **Headings:** Rockwell or Courier (typewriter/diner sign style)
- **Body:** American Typewriter or Courier (nostalgic, readable)

### Visual Style
- Bold geometric shapes and borders
- Retro badges, ribbons, and banners
- Checkered patterns (gingham, tablecloths)
- Vintage illustration accents (wheat stalks, rolling pins)
- High-contrast color blocking
- **Vibe:** State fair posters, classic diner menus, vintage recipe cards

## Theme 4: **Heritage Classic**
*Timeless elegance, fine dining*

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** #1B3A4B (Navy Blue)
- **Secondary:** #B8860B (Dark Goldenrod)
- **Accent:** #8B0000 (Dark Red/Burgundy)
- **Text:** #000000 (Pure Black)
- **Background:** #FAF9F6 (Antique White)
- **Highlight:** #E5D4B5 (Champagne)

### Typography
- **Display:** Baskerville or Garamond (refined, traditional)
- **Headings:** Trajan Pro or Cinzel (classical Roman capitals)
- **Body:** Minion Pro or Adobe Caslon (elegant, readable)

### Visual Style
- Symmetrical layouts with centered text
- Gold foil accents and ornate borders
- Rich, moody photography with dramatic lighting
- Classical ornamental elements (filigree, crests)
- Formal plating and presentation
- **Vibe:** Heirloom cookbook, fine dining, presidential library
