## Prompt 1 --- Signature Midwest Table Cover

Cookbook cover image, portrait orientation. Rustic farmhouse wooden
table filled with Midwestern comfort foods: cast iron hotdish, apple
pie, cornbread, pickled vegetables in mason jars, fresh butter, and a
loaf of homemade bread. Golden natural window light from the side, linen
cloth accents, wheat stalks in background, warm amber and muted sage
color palette. Clean space at top for title text. Professional food
photography, shallow depth of field, cozy Midwest mood.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Prompt 2 --- Midwest Food Map Concept

Cookbook cover illustration-style food photography, portrait layout.
Overhead view of the Midwest region formed from food ingredients: corn,
cheese curds, wild rice, cherries, sausages, apples, bread, and pies
arranged into a stylized map on a weathered wood background. Soft
natural lighting, warm wheat tones, muted sage accents. Centered
composition with negative space for title. Playful but refined cookbook
cover aesthetic.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Prompt 3 --- Church Basement Potluck Scene

Cookbook cover photo, portrait orientation. Long potluck table in a
church basement filled with Midwest dishes: casseroles, deviled eggs,
fried chicken, dinner rolls, pies, and Jell-O salad. Multiple serving
dishes on a simple tablecloth. Warm indoor lighting, nostalgic and
inviting atmosphere. Rustic color grading with ambers and soft reds.
Foreground in sharp focus, background softly blurred. Space for title at
top.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Prompt 4 --- Farm-to-Table Ingredient Still Life

Minimalist cookbook cover image, portrait orientation. Farm-to-table
still life with Midwest ingredients arranged neatly: ears of corn, brown
eggs, flour in wooden bowl, apples, cheese wedge, wheat stalks on
weathered wood surface. Soft directional natural light, modern editorial
food photography. Warm wheat colors with muted sage background tones.
Simple composition with negative space for title. Premium cookbook
aesthetic.
