## Ingredients

- 1 large yellow onion, diced
- 2 medium carrots, peeled and diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tablespoons tomato paste

## Instructions

1. **SautÃ© the aromatics.** In a large Dutch oven or heavy pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add onion and carrots. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 8â€“10 minutes until softened and the onion is translucent.

2. **Add garlic and tomato paste.** Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly, to caramelize it slightly and deepen the flavor.

3. **Add tomatoes and broth.** Pour in the canned tomatoes with their juices, crushing them with your hands or a spoon as you add them. Add broth, sugar, salt, pepper, dried basil, oregano, and bay leaf. Stir to combine.

4. **Simmer.** Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Simmer uncovered for 20â€“25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the carrots are very tender and the flavors have melded.

5. **Remove bay leaf and blend.** Discard the bay leaf. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup directly in the pot until completely smooth. (Alternatively, carefully transfer in batches to a blender, blend until smooth, and return to the pot.)

6. **Add the cream.** Reduce heat to low. Stir in heavy cream until fully incorporated. Taste and adjust seasoning â€” add more sugar if too acidic, more salt if flat.

7. **Serve.** Ladle into bowls. Drizzle with a swirl of cream, scatter fresh basil on top, and serve immediately with grilled cheese sandwiches for dipping.

## Tips & Variations

- **San Marzano Tomatoes:** These Italian plum tomatoes are sweeter and less acidic than regular canned tomatoes. They make a noticeable difference. If unavailable, use the best quality whole peeled tomatoes you can find.
- **Roasted Tomato Version:** In summer, use 3 pounds fresh tomatoes, halved and roasted at 400Â°F for 30 minutes until caramelized. Proceed with the recipe.
- **Dairy-Free:** Substitute coconut cream for heavy cream, or omit entirely for a lighter soup.
- **Smoky Twist:** Add Â½ teaspoon smoked paprika and a chipotle pepper in adobo.
- **Grilled Cheese Croutons:** Cut grilled cheese sandwiches into cubes and float them on top.
- **Slow Cooker:** Combine all ingredients except cream in a slow cooker. Cook on low for 6â€“8 hours. Blend, then stir in cream.
- **Freezer Friendly:** Freeze without the cream. When reheating, warm the soup and stir in fresh cream.
